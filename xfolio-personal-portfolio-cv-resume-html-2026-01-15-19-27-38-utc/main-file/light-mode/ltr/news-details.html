Design is Problem-Solving

At its core, design is about solving problems. Whether it's helping users navigate a website, making a mobile app more intuitive, or creating a visual identity that captures the soul of a brand, every project begins with a need. That need could be to communicate something more clearly, to encourage action, or to deliver a better experience.
